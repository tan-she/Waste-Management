Learn Waste Segregation

Understanding proper waste segregation is the first step towards a sustainable future. Learn how to categorize your waste correctly.

Organic Waste

Food scraps, garden waste, and other biodegradable materials.

  • Fruit and vegetable peels
  • Food leftovers
  • Garden trimmings
  • Tea bags and coffee grounds

Recyclable Waste

Materials that can be processed and reused.

  • Paper and cardboard
  • Plastic bottles and containers
  • Glass bottles and jars
  • Metal cans and foil

Hazardous Waste

Materials that require special handling and disposal.

  • Batteries
  • Electronic waste
  • Chemicals and cleaning products
  • Medical waste

Residual Waste

Non-recyclable and non-hazardous waste.

  • Diapers and sanitary products
  • Broken ceramics
  • Used tissues and paper towels
  • Mixed material packaging
